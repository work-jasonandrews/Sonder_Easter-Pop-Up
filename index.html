“Food is the memory of a place you never want to leave.”

Hey! I’m Jason — and honestly, this all started because I just love feeding people. I grew up around a table that was always full — full of food, full of noise, full of people who’d drop by unannounced and somehow always end up staying for dinner. That feeling of warmth, of someone taking time to cook something just for you — that’s what I want to bring to your Easter this year. Everything I make is from scratch, cooked in my own kitchen, with ingredients I’d use for my own family. No shortcuts, no preservatives, no nonsense. Just real food, made with real care. This is a small, personal pop-up — so every order genuinely means a lot to me. I’d love for you to come collect if you can. Let’s put faces to names. 🤴

— Jason
